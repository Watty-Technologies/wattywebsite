How it works

Getting a Watty Powerbank is easy!

  1. First, make sure you have downloaded the app: Download
  2. Set up your preferred subscription in the app
  3. Use the map in the app to find the nearest Watty Powerbank Station
  4. When at the station, scan the QR code
  5. Pick up your new Watty Powerbank

When your Watty Powerbank runs out of juice, just visit a Watty Powerbank Station and get a new fully charged one as long as your subscription is active!
